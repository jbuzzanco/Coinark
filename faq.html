Frequently asked questions

  • Q: Do I need a digital wallet to use a Coinark Bitcoin ATM?
    A: Yes, you will need a digital wallet to store your Bitcoin after purchasing it from a Coinark Bitcoin ATM. You can download a free digital wallet app on your smartphone whether you have an iPhone or Android phone. Learn how in our article on crypto wallets.
    You can also have a wallet printed out for you at our ATMs. It will print out a QR Code that you can scan for future purchases. This is only if you don't have a smartphone, but you must keep this wallet safe and treat it as cash, because if you lose the printed wallet, the Bitcoin is also lost, as you would with cash.
  • Q: Is it safe to use a Coinark Bitcoin ATM?
    A: Yes, Coinark Bitcoin ATMs are secure and reliable, with many built-in safety features to protect your transaction and personal information.
  • Q: Can I buy other cryptocurrencies besides Bitcoin from a Coinark Bitcoin ATMs?
    A: Not yet, but it can be a feature. To show your interest, please sign up for our email and text updates, and contact us to let us know you are interested.  Coinark Bitcoin ATMs may in the future allow you to buy other cryptocurrencies besides Bitcoin, such as Ethereum or Litecoin.

    A: Yes, there is typically a limit to how much Bitcoin you can buy from a Coinark Bitcoin ATM, usually around $10,000 per transaction. If you are planning on buying more than $5,000 in one purchase, you can call us at (814) 480-0868 beforehand to assure that your bitcoin purchase will go as quickly as possible. Please note for larger transactions of any purchase of bitcoin it can take a day or 2 to be complete due to regulations but you will receive your bitcoin so long as everything you provide is correct.
  • Q: Can I sell Bitcoin at a Coinark Bitcoin ATM?
    A: Coinark Bitcoin ATMs allow you to sell Bitcoin as well as buy it. For larger sell orders, please call us at (814) 480-0868 beforehand to assure that your bitcoin sell order will go through as quickly as possible. The amount you can sell may vary depending upon the amount of cash (USD) in each machine.
  • Q: Do I need to provide personal information to use a Coinark Bitcoin ATM?
    A: It depends on the machine and the amount of Bitcoin you are buying. Regulations require us to collect some a certain amounts. See more of the information required on the home page.
  • Q: Are there any age restrictions for using a Coinark Bitcoin ATM?
    A: Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to use a Coinark Bitcoin ATM.
